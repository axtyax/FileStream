How to transfer files quickly between a Windows PC and a Mac


When sending files between different devices or operating systems, our first instinct is usually to send them via email. While this can work in some cases, we often have to send files that go over a size limit of less than a gigabyte. Not to mention how much of a hassle it can be to have to log into Gmail, email the recipient, have them log in on their device, and download the file.

Online services make it somewhat easier, but still impose a size limit of a few gigabytes and require you to enter personal details like your email address. Transferring files from one device to another doesn't need to be this difficult.

FileStream makes it easy to transfer files of any size between any two devices.
All you have to do is go to the site, click 'Add Files', choose the files you want to send, and hit 'Upload'.
Then just send the download link to the recipient, and they can download the file with just one click.
